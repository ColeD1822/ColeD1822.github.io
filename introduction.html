Introduction to Me!

My name is Cole Dennis and I am a student at the University of Missouri. I am Majoring in IT and I love computers. During the pandemic that started in 2020, I helped people in Kansas City build computers that needed them for work and school since everything was done online. Building computers and helping people was something that I enjoyed so much that I knew a career in IT was perfect for me. I plan to continue my education at the University of Missouri and find an Internship that suits me!


I have a lot of experience with computers and I am constantly learning new things about them everyday. It fascinates me how much technology and careers change around the tech industry. My main goal is to contribute and help make a difference wherever my career takes me!
